Data source:

We use the live COVID19 data from the GitHub data repository for the 2019 Novel Coronavirus Visual Dashboard by the Johns Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering (JHU CSSE).

Notes:

On April 17th, China outbreak city Wuhan raised death toll by 50% (BBC). As a consequence, outliers of this type were removed ― values whose distance from the mean is larger than 5 \(\sigma\) are replaced by the previous-day value plus/minus the mean of the previous 7-day direct differences depending on whether the trend is rising/falling off.
